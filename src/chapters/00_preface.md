# Preface

This book began with a simple question:

What if identity is not primarily about space, but about time?

Modern frameworks explain language, culture, religion, and genetics.
But they fail to explain continuity.

This work introduces two concepts:

- Chronostate
- Chrononation

Together, they redefine identity as a temporal structure.
